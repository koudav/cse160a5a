right click + hold to pan, left click + hold to rotate, scroll wheel to zoom

Welcome to Planet Gazing 101. You have been given binoculars. Press 'b' to toggle them. They don't zoom in very far, and they're very heavy for some reason, so you can't move or look around while you have them on. We ran out of budget for telescopes.

For whatever reason, sometimes the camera controls become unresponsive and don't move very far while click and dragging. I have found double clicking before dragging to help with this, or refreshing the page.
